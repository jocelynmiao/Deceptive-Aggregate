The Deceptive Global Aggregate

Writeup

1. What have you done so far?

Currently, we have formulated the progression in which our slides will take from one visualization to another. Seen currently, we have also established a baseline of these slides that operate on a re-rendering system, although in the final product we would like if the transition was smoother, more seamless, and animated. We also have some early implementations of animation and interactivity, although we will likely overhaul it so these features are not as demanding on the user to trigger. We currently look forward to fully implementing the slides before we work on fine tuning the user experience, as well as effectively fleshing out the narrative / perspective we are trying to communicate.

2.What will be the most challenging part of your project to design and why?

Currently the challenging hurdle of completely creating this project is connecting each slide in a visually appealing manner and contextually connecting them together in a story like format. These will be the most challenging parts because it requires a lot of thoughtful design choices that trickle down into creating a cohesive webpage to interact with and digest. Additionally, it is also going to be a challenge to choose the right visual encodings that make our webpage as least overwhelming as possible on audiences, especially when a lot of data - possibly in various representations - are being presented. We would also like to make the progression through the slides as hands off as possible, as the manual clicking required adds a demand of the viewer.
